Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get tested?
Join our Discord server and request a test in the appropriate channel for your region and gamemode. Make sure to read the testing requirements first.
What are the tier requirements?
Each tier has specific skill requirements that vary by gamemode. Check our whitepaper for detailed criteria and testing procedures.
Can I appeal my tier result?
Yes, appeals can be submitted through our Discord support system within 48 hours of your test. Provide evidence and reasoning for your appeal.
How does the points system work?
Points are earned through testing, tournaments, and achievements. Combat Grandmaster requires 400+ points, Combat Master requires 300+ points.
